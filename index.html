Another Text Adventure?

How I decided to combine geo-mapping with plain fun

Mapping the New York City Subway

One of the things that has captivated me the most about NYC is the one of the most obvious pieces of urban infrastructure in plain sight, its transit system. One of the few 24/7 transit systems in the world, it is used and depended upon for millions' transit needs.

Made for everyone.

I keep both the tourist and resident in mind. Locations are described both verbally and with music, but extra info. is given for those who live here and want to find out more. An easy and hard mode will be out soon(you better play hard if you're a New Yorker!)

Packed with features.

Quests will be added shortly. Eventually I'll expand to other cities and allow users to create their own transit system or edit existing ones.
